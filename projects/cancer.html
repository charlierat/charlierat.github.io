Cancer Detection Algorithm

Revolutionizing early cancer detection through advanced image processing. This algorithm assists medical professionals in identifying potential lung cancer nodules, potentially saving thousands of lives through early intervention.

How It Works

1

preprocessing()

Advanced noise reduction and image enhancement using multiple filtering techniques to prepare CT scans for accurate analysis.

  • Gaussian filter for background noise reduction
  • Median filter eliminates salt-and-pepper artifacts
  • Non-local-means preserves crucial edge details
2

lungSegmentation()

Intelligent isolation of lung regions using advanced segmentation algorithms to focus analysis on relevant anatomical areas.

  • Automatic lung boundary detection
  • Region of interest isolation
  • Anatomical structure preservation
3

noduleDetection()

Sophisticated pattern recognition to identify potential cancerous nodules based on density, shape, and texture characteristics.

  • Density-based candidate identification
  • Shape and morphology analysis
  • False positive reduction algorithms
4

featureExtraction()

Comprehensive analysis of nodule characteristics to determine malignancy risk using multiple quantitative features.

  • Area calculation and size assessment
  • Calcification percentage analysis
  • Shape irregularity and solidity metrics
  • Texture and density pattern evaluation
5

riskAssessment()

Machine learning-powered risk calculation that prioritizes nodules by their probability of malignancy for medical review.

  • Multi-feature risk scoring algorithm
  • Probability ranking and prioritization
  • Clinical decision support output
