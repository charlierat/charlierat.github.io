Rendering Engine · C#

Multithreaded Ray Tracing Engine

A CPU path-traced renderer written from scratch — full Wavefront OBJ/MTL parsing, Phong illumination, texture mapping with bilinear filtering, and per-vertex normals for smooth shading. A custom Bounding Volume Hierarchy cuts render times to under 60 seconds for 330,000+ triangle scenes at 2048×2048. The panel on the right is a miniature GPU port of the same algorithm, ray tracing live at 60fps.

Computer Vision · Accessibility

Duane Syndrome Vision Simulator

A real-time computer-vision application that lets anyone experience the world through the eyes of someone with Duane syndrome, a rare eye-movement disorder. Live camera feeds are transformed with Gaussian filtering, pixel shifting, and mask-based blending to recreate diplopia (double vision) and peripheral loss — built as an empathy and education tool for clinicians. The panel simulates the effect live.
